Privacy Policy

App: MoneyDrain  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2025  ·  Developer: Convenience Tools

1. Introduction

Convenience Tools ("we", "us", or "our") built MoneyDrain as a freemium app. This page explains what information is involved when you use our app, where it goes, and how it is protected.

By using MoneyDrain, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data You Enter (Stored Locally Only)

MoneyDrain is a manual subscription tracker. Everything you type stays on your device — it is never transmitted to our servers. This includes:

  • Subscription details: name, amount, category, billing cycle, dates, trial info, and notes
  • Usage logs: dates you tap "I used this today" for each subscription
  • App preferences: currency, language, theme, and notification settings

This data is stored in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). If you delete the app, all of this data is deleted with it.

3. Device Data Used Temporarily

The following is read by the app at startup but is never stored or transmitted:

  • Device locale — read once at launch to auto-detect your language preference
  • Notification permission status — checked to determine whether to send you billing reminders. Not shared with anyone.

4. Third-Party Services: RevenueCat

The only external data flow in MoneyDrain is through RevenueCat, which we use to manage in-app purchases and premium entitlements. RevenueCat is contacted when you:

  • Open the app (to check your premium status)
  • View the upgrade paywall
  • Make a purchase or restore a previous purchase

RevenueCat may receive a device identifier, app store receipt data, and purchase history. Your subscription data (e.g. Netflix, Spotify) is never sent to RevenueCat — only the information needed to verify your purchase entitlement.

For full details on how RevenueCat handles data, please review their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Summary

Data Where it goes
Subscription names, amounts, dates Device only (never transmitted)
Usage logs Device only (never transmitted)
App preferences Device only (never transmitted)
Device locale Read at startup only — not stored or transmitted
In-app purchase status RevenueCat (see Section 4)

There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporting, no ad tracking, and no backend server. The only network calls the app makes are RevenueCat purchase checks.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties, except for RevenueCat as described in Section 4, which is necessary to operate the premium features of the app.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access any personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

Because MoneyDrain stores all subscription data locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights simply by deleting the app. For data held by RevenueCat (purchase history), you may contact them directly via their privacy policy. For any other requests, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

MoneyDrain is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has used the app, you can remove all local data by deleting the app from the device.

9. Security

Because all subscription data remains on your device, its security depends on your device's own security measures. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date. Purchase data handled by RevenueCat is protected according to their own security standards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
